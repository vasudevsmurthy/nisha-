Life Experience in Vladikavkaz

First Impression of the City

When I first arrived in Vladikavkaz, I was immediately struck by the breathtaking view of the mountains surrounding the city, and the pleasant atmosphere. The people were warm and welcoming, and I noticed a beautiful mix of traditional and modern buildings throughout the area. Hearing the Russian language spoken everywhere was both fascinating and motivating as I was encouraged to learn Russian. Vladikavkaz is a lively urban city, and I'm glad that the NOSMA campus is located right at its heart, offering a lively and enriching environment for students.

Daily Life and Routine

I live in a comfortable hostel near the academy, which makes it easy to manage my daily routine. Each morning, I travel to the academy for classes. In the evening, I enjoy a short break for studying or reviewing what I've learned. Meals with friends provide a nice break during the day, and in the evening, I like to go for a walk or visit the gym to relax and stay active. During my free time, I often visit nearby supermarkets to buy essentials, relax in local cafes with classmates, or spend time in the city's peaceful parks and along the river. These moments help me balance my academic life with enjoyable experiences in Vladikavkaz.

Culture and Food

Living in Vladikavkaz has given me a wonderful chance to experience both Russian and Ossetian culture. I enjoy taking part in local festivals and observing traditional customs that reflect the warmth and hospitality of the people. Trying local dishes has been an exciting experience, with my favorite being Ossetian pies, which are delicious! I've also grown to love the same kind of food. I love cooking Indian food with my friends, which helps me share a taste of home and exchange our cultures through food.

Challenges and Adaptation

At first, adjusting to life in Vladikavkaz was a bit challenging because of the cold winters, the language barrier, and being far from my family. However, with time, I learned to adapt by picking up basic Russian phrases, which made daily communication easier. I also made new friends and participated in activities, which helped me feel more connected and comfortable in my new environment.

Life in Vladikavkaz

Vladikavkaz, the capital of North Ossetia, is a beautiful city nestled in the Caucasus Mountains. Living here has been an adventure, with stunning mountain views, rich culture, and warm people.
